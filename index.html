Automated Plant Watering System

Full IoT system built with Arduino Uno and MATLAB that autonomously monitors soil moisture and temperature in real time. When soil voltage drops below 1V (dry threshold), the system activates a water pump, flashes a red LED, and triggers a buzzer alert. A green LED confirms wet soil and stops the pump. Features a live-updating dual graph (moisture vs. time, temperature vs. time), a manual stop button, and data logging every 10 seconds — designed to make plant care easier, especially for seniors.
